# 📐 PHASE 1: Algebra Reset (Weeks 1–3)

## 🎯 Goal
Rebuild algebra foundation required for ML.

## 📚 Resources
- Khan Academy Algebra Playlist
- Organic Chemistry Tutor (YouTube)

## Topics to Master
- Solving linear equations
- Exponents
- Logarithms
- Functions (f(x))
- Graph intuition
- Factoring polynomials
- Expanding expressions

## You Should Be Able To:
- Rearrange equations confidently
- Understand slope
- Evaluate functions
- Apply log rules
- Simplify expressions

# 📊 PHASE 2: Linear Algebra (Weeks 4–6)

## 📘 Book
Introduction to Linear Algebra — Gilbert Strang

## 📺 Supplement
- 3Blue1Brown: Essence of Linear Algebra
- MIT OpenCourseWare (Strang Lectures)

## Topics to Master
- Vectors
- Dot product
- Matrix multiplication
- Linear combinations
- Span
- Systems of equations (Ax = b)
- Linear transformations
- Column space & row space
- Eigenvalues & eigenvectors (basic intuition)

## You Should Understand:
- Why matrix multiplication works
- Why order matters
- What span means geometrically
- Why neural networks use matrices
